Summary

Applied economics translated into decision-ready analytics.

Applied economist and development data analyst working across public policy, poverty, labor, social protection, financial inclusion and productive transformation. I use SQL, Python, R and Stata to clean, validate and analyze household surveys, administrative records and country-year panels, then translate findings into dashboards, reports and stakeholder-ready communication. My public portfolio emphasizes reproducibility, data-quality checks, privacy-aware documentation and clear limitations for policy, research and analytics audiences.
