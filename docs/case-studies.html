Case Study 1 - BI and finance

Inclusive Credit Risk Analytics Bolivia

Problem: credit and inclusion teams need a clear view of portfolio risk, stress signals and executive KPIs.

Users: BI recruiter, financial inclusion team, risk analytics reviewer.

Data: public-facing aggregate analytical outputs and privacy-reviewed documentation.

Workflow: portfolio diagnostics, KPI design, risk segmentation, forecasting/stress communication, validation and reporting.

Tools: R, Quarto, GitHub Pages, SQL-ready documentation; Power BI is planned.

Outputs: public analytical report, dashboard-style report, repository and validation/provenance documentation.

Key finding: demonstrates financial analytics and executive communication without exposing private data.

Limitation: Power BI remains planned until a public artifact, export or documented screenshots exist.

Case Study 2 - Development analytics

Poverty, Informality and Social Protection in LAC

Problem: policy teams need comparable indicators linking poverty, labor informality and social protection coverage.

Users: development data analyst recruiter, policy team, research data analyst.

Data: public country-year analytical products, validation outputs, figures, tables and dashboard artifacts.

Workflow: multi-source integration, country-year panel construction, missingness review, validation and dashboard communication.

Tools: Python, HTML, GitHub Pages and validation reports; Tableau is planned.

Outputs: public page, dashboard, figures, tables and analytical artifact catalog.

Key finding: shows development analytics with transparent validation and policy-oriented navigation.

Limitation: Tableau storytelling remains planned until a real workbook, story, export or screenshots exist.

Case Study 3 - Applied research

Economic Complexity and Structural Transformation in LAC

Problem: research and policy audiences need to understand productive capabilities and diversification constraints.

Users: research data analyst recruiter, development economist, international organization reviewer.

Data: public derived country-product-year outputs, Product Space diagnostics, validation files, paper, policy brief and DOI metadata.

Workflow: RCA, ECI, PCI, Product Space proximity, opportunity screening, data-quality validation and research communication.

Tools: R, Shiny, Quarto, SQL contracts and DuckDB-ready data model.

Outputs: paper, policy brief, interactive explorer, SQL contracts and Zenodo DOI.

Key finding: demonstrates advanced applied economics, network analytics and cautious policy interpretation.

Limitation: opportunity scores are screening tools; fixed-effects estimates are observational associations.
