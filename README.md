## About

I am an applied economist working with quantitative methods for public policy, inclusive development and research communication. My work connects economic questions with practical analytical systems: survey and administrative-data workflows, econometric modelling, spatial analysis, financial inclusion diagnostics, monitoring and evaluation, and dashboards for decision support. I use R, Python, Stata, SQL, Quarto, GitHub and BI tools to make evidence easier to inspect, reproduce and discuss. The portfolio is organized by research capabilities rather than by employers: each project is framed by a policy question, a method, a reproducible workflow and a clear interpretation of what the evidence can and cannot say. The guiding principle is modest but demanding: useful analysis should be transparent, documented, visually clear and honest about limitations.

### Research Labs

#### Applied Economics Lab

Purpose: study poverty, labor markets, financial development, structural transformation, public investment and productive capabilities.

Methods: applied econometrics, panel data, decomposition, indicator systems, policy diagnostics.

Explore: [economic complexity](https://github.com/MonicaCT/economic-complexity-structural-transformation-lac), [financial development](https://github.com/MonicaCT/latin-america-financial-development-lab), [poverty and social protection](https://github.com/MonicaCT/poverty-informality-social-protection-lac), [spatial education methods](https://github.com/MonicaCT/SpatialEconometrics_education).

#### Research Methods Lab

Purpose: develop and document methods for econometrics, spatial analysis, robustness, impact evaluation and modelling.

Methods: spatial econometrics, panel models, matching, forecasting, model validation, Social Accounting Matrix methods.

Explore: [SpatialEconometrics_education](https://github.com/MonicaCT/SpatialEconometrics_education), [SAMmultiplayer](https://github.com/MonicaCT/SAMmultiplayer), [data science requirements](https://github.com/MonicaCT/data-science-requirements-breast-cancer).

#### Development Analytics Lab

Purpose: translate social and economic data into evidence for inclusive development, vulnerability analysis, monitoring and public policy.

Methods: survey analytics, territorial indicators, dashboards, reproducible reporting, risk and vulnerability diagnostics.

Explore: [poverty and social protection](https://github.com/MonicaCT/poverty-informality-social-protection-lac), [inclusive credit risk](https://github.com/MonicaCT/InclusiveCreditRiskAnalytics-Bolivia), [structural vulnerability](https://github.com/MonicaCT/structural-vulnerability-lac-research), [rural housing](https://github.com/MonicaCT/rural-bolivia-housing-analytics).

#### Business Intelligence Lab

Purpose: design dashboards, KPI systems and executive reporting that support decisions without hiding methodological limits.

Methods: dashboard design, reporting pipelines, KPI frameworks, data storytelling.

Explore: [Power BI portfolio](https://github.com/MonicaCT/portafolio_powerBI), [credit risk analytics](https://monicact.github.io/InclusiveCreditRiskAnalytics-Bolivia/), [financial development dashboard](https://monicact.github.io/latin-america-financial-development-lab/).

Purpose: build reusable workflows for data processing, validation, modelling, automation and communication.

Explore: [data-science requirements](https://github.com/MonicaCT/data-science-requirements-breast-cancer), [rural housing analytics](https://github.com/MonicaCT/rural-bolivia-housing-analytics), [economic complexity pipeline](https://github.com/MonicaCT/economic-complexity-structural-transformation-lac).
